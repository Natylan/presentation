Presentation

Good evening. I would like to introduce myself
I am Natalia. I come from the world of art, design and composition. I learned to paint marbled paper as a self-taught. I have worked as a bookbinder and paper decorator for bookbinders. Currently I live with my partner in Portugal. We are both immersed in the path of transfer to the digital world and programming. Gradually I have felt the call to enter the digital world and programming. And that's why I am here.

Goals

My highest goals:
are to create what does not yet exist in existing programs, but has already emerged as ideas in my head. for example: create the marble pan to digitally paint paper.
But following the chronology of the logical steps. As they say, you have to start at the beginning.
short-term goals:
become a web developer and work in that field

My latest achievements

Finding a very good teacher who has been guiding and supporting me for the last 7 months, with her I am learning logic and programming in python.
She showed me the challanges of CODEWARS, HACHERRANK, she introduced me to the world of github.
Since 2015 when I discovered the domestika platform, I started with calligraphy, aquarelle and painting courses, to gradually move on to the digital world, continuing with potoshop, illustrator, until my last purchases were wordpress courses, online business.
I am currently doing a course to learn responsive web, but they are still very basic.
I won three PRO months to study freely in CODECADEMY which I used to complement my fundamentals in python, and a basic introduction to command lines.
I am currently enrolled in a program with the people of Lewagon Lisbon, I have also won participation in this workshop, divided into 8 sessions to learn how to make a landing page and a program to learn how to create a user account.
I have also signed up for a webinar with the people of ironhack for the creation of web pages, but it is two sessions of two hours only.

Conclusions

As you can see, my interest in this world is growing.
Sometimes it seems to me that one can easily get lost in so much information and that unnecessary time can be wasted learning something. That with a guide like the intensive 9-week ironhack program it would be solved without distractions. At this point of doing everything on my own, I think it would be a win for me to access your bootcamp and head over to. and for you, because I am a person who has a lot of interest, energy and time available to dedicate to it.

What is your greatest strength/weakness?

I would say that my greatest strength is my tenacity. When I decide to do something, I want to do it well, so I am willing to put in the time and effort to do it. Perhaps it is not just about doing it well, but about satisfying a hunger that comes from within me, if that hunger is appeased, it gives me satisfaction, and that is what I want to share with the world
I would say that my weakness is that sometimes I get obsessed with things, which makes me lose perspective, and without it the most insignificant obstacles could turn into problems. Luckily in the last time I am practicing meditation, which makes me get out of the temporary obsession to look at things from a new point of view
